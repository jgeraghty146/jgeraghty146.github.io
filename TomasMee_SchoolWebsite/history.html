St. Gerald's College Gaelic Football

HOME | EVENTS | HISTORY

St. Gerald’s DLS College is synonymous with Gaelic Football. The school has been successful across all grades winning numerous Connacht Leagues and Championships. In recent years, we have seen a surge in the number of St. Gerald’s DLS College students representing Mayo at various age levels, with a sizeable proportion moving to the senior ranks; Aidan O’Shea, Seamus O’Shea, Cillian O’Connor, Paddy Durcan, James Durcan and Robert Hennelly, to name but a few.
