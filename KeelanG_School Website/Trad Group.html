The Trad Group for St Geralds is pretty well known around the school.

The School has used the trad group on multible instances, they perform annually in the Recital in the Linen Hall

Traditional instrumetal musci group or Trad Group is a series of muscians playing Traditional Irish instruments, such as

Fiddle, Accordian, Flute, Banjo, Guitar, Harp and Bouzouki
